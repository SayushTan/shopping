1. Who are we
Women's Craft is a socially responsible business dedicated to uplift the disadvantaged women. After a decade experience in promoting disafvantage women in small business.
Women's Craft was established in January 2006 (2062 Magh 19) under the act of Nepal as a sister concerns of Women's Creation.

2. What are we for
Empowering women through providing economic opportunities and independence is out mission.
With a view to contribute to the mission, Women's Craft has the following objecives:

| To organize group of womens producers, entrepreneurs
| To enhance entrepreneurial capacity and competence of women producers / entrepreneurs
| To facilitate collective marketing products of womens prodicers / entrepreneurs



Following are the major instruments of the Women's Craft promoting women in businesses:

| Provision of market oriented vocational skill development acivities
| Provision of poduct development and design and business skill training
| Collective marketing of their products
| Helping to venture on the projects of income generation

3. What we have to offer you
Women's Craft have been supporting the goup of 200 women from  peri-urban and rural area of Nepal in collective marketing of their products such as Herbals, Jewelries, Christmas Ornaments, Hand bags, Mithila Arts Products, Natural Fabrics, Clothing, Sikki, and Bamboo Accessories and many more...
